SmartGrant helps Singapore SMEs access a wide range of government grants. Learn about the key grants and see if your business qualifies.

Key Singapore SME Grants

Our platform currently covers these major grant programs, with more being added regularly.

Productivity Solutions Grant (PSG)

Supports companies in the adoption of pre-scoped IT solutions and equipment to enhance business processes.

Maximum Funding:

Up to 70% of costs

Key Eligibility Criteria:

  • Registered and operating in Singapore
  • Purchase/lease/subscription of the IT solutions or equipment must be used in Singapore
  • Have at least 30% local shareholding

Enterprise Development Grant (EDG)

Helps Singapore companies grow and transform, supporting projects in innovation, productivity, and internationalization.

Maximum Funding:

Up to 70% of costs

Key Eligibility Criteria:

  • Registered and operating in Singapore
  • Have at least 30% local shareholding
  • Be financially viable and have a clear growth strategy

Market Readiness Assistance (MRA)

Supports SMEs in their overseas expansion by helping to defray costs of internationalization.

Maximum Funding:

Up to 70% of eligible costs, capped at S$100,000 per company per fiscal year

Key Eligibility Criteria:

  • Business entity registered/incorporated in Singapore
  • Have at least 30% local shareholding
  • Group annual sales turnover ≤ S$100 million or group employment ≤ 200 employees

Understanding Eligibility

Grant eligibility can be complex, but SmartGrant simplifies the process by automatically matching your business with grants you qualify for.

Common Eligibility Criteria

While each grant has specific requirements, most Singapore government grants share these basic eligibility criteria:

Registration & Incorporation

Your business must be registered and operating in Singapore.

Local Shareholding

Most grants require at least 30% local shareholding.

Financial Requirements

Your company typically needs to demonstrate financial viability and may need to meet certain revenue or employee count criteria.

Project Requirements

The proposed project must align with the grant's objectives and be implemented in Singapore.

Technology or Innovation Focus

Many grants prioritize projects that adopt new technologies, innovative processes, or productivity enhancements.
